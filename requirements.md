## 1. Project Overview

AI Content Copilot for Growth is an AI-driven platform that helps creators, startups, and small businesses automate content creation, personalization, scheduling, and engagement analysis across digital platforms.

The system transforms a single idea or business description into:
- Social media posts
- Optimized captions and hashtags
- Content calendar
- Engagement insights

---

## 2. Problem Statement

Users struggle with:
- Consistent content creation
- Knowing what content performs best
- Platform-specific formatting
- Time-consuming analytics

Manual marketing is expensive and inefficient for small creators.

---

## 3. Objectives

- Automate content generation
- Personalize posts for different platforms
- Improve engagement using AI feedback
- Reduce content creation time
- Enable data-driven growth

---

## 4. Target Users

- Social media creators
- Small business owners
- Students building brands
- Marketing freelancers
- Startups

---

## 5. Core Features

### 5.1 Content Generation
- Input: topic/business type
- Output:
  - Instagram post
  - LinkedIn post
  - Twitter/X post
  - Captions + hashtags

### 5.2 AI Content Planner
- Weekly content calendar
- Suggested themes
- Best posting time

### 5.3 Content Optimization
- Platform-specific tone
- Keyword optimization
- Length adjustment

### 5.4 Engagement Analytics
- Performance tracking
- Best post prediction
- Audience sentiment analysis

### 5.5 Feedback Learning System
- AI improves future content based on engagement
